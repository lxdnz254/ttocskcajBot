___Movement___
Try inspecting some items.
```>inspect bed```
Try moving through doors or windows.
```>enter {door_name}```

___Items___
View inventory
```>inventory```
Take Item
```>take {item_name}```
Equip Item (Must be in inventory)
```>equip {item_name}```
See currently equiped item
```>equip```

___Attacking___
Attack creature (with equiped item)
```>attack {creature_name}```

___Stats___
View stats, such as health etc
```>stats```
